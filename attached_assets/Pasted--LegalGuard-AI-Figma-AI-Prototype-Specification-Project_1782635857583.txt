# Product Overview

LegalGuard AI is an AI-powered legal assistant designed specifically for law firms and enterprises. Unlike traditional AI assistants, LegalGuard AI introduces a governance layer called ArmorIQ that blocks sensitive actions until human approval is received. Every action is cryptographically logged, creating an immutable audit trail and verification receipt.

The UI should feel modern, premium, enterprise-grade, trustworthy, minimal, and security-first.
